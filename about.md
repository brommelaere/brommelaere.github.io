Welcome to my page where I blog about data science, data visualization, and economics. The purpose of this blog is to showcase projects I am working on, create a repository of concepts and code for working with specific types of data, and to teach techniques to those who are just begining their data science journey. My projects focus on data of personal interest or business ideas that I have and am prototyping. I grew up a basketball stats junkie - and there are several projects in the pipeline analyzing basketball data. 

Who am I? I am an Economist by training, with an MA from one of Canada's top Economics programs and one year of my PhD in Economics completed. I have  extensive experience in econometric analysis, data visualization, statistical modelling, market research, and cost-benefit analysis. Having worked in data intensive industries, I have worked with complex, large datasets which has affoded me with vast experience programming data solutions with R, Python, VBA, STATA, and SQL. Having worked as a consultant in both antitrust economics and infrastructure analysis, I have had the pleasure to work with clients in myriad industries including: financial intermediation, natural resources, electricity production, chemicals, telecommunications, retailing, warehousing, construction, and transportation. 

### Notable Achievements 

My background includes numerous awards for academic achievements, multiple leadership roles, and working with non-profits to improve their best practices and increase their fundraising initiatives using data driven approaches.
